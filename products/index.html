The System

Focus when it counts. Restore when it's done.

Two evidence-based nootropics built around your circadian rhythm — one for daytime clarity and calm under pressure, one for the evening return to presence. Not more stimulation. Better timing.

Why Two?

Your brain doesn't run one mode all day. Your supplement shouldn't either.

Mornings ask you to produce, focus, and hold it together under pressure. Evenings ask you to come down, let go, and actually be present. Those are two different jobs for your nervous system — and no single pill does both well.

Most "all-in-one" formulas pick one and fake the other. BalanceEQ splits the day on purpose: EQ:Focus feeds daytime cognition — sharper focus, a steadier stress response, cellular energy — without the caffeine spike-and-crash.

EQ:Restore handles the part nobody formulates for: the transition out of work mode and back into presence, so the evening actually feels like yours. Same system, two halves. That's the whole idea.
