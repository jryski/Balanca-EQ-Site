Citicoline: Attention & Focus

Our Dose: 250mg (Cognizin®)

Supports working memory, attention, and cognitive function

Research:

  • Cognizin® improves attention and psychomotor speed (2023)
  • Citicoline enhances cognitive function (2012)

Magnesium L-Threonate: Cognitive Support & Recovery

Our Dose: 1,000mg (Magtein®)

Supports the evening transition, memory consolidation, and brain magnesium levels

Research:

  • Mag L-threonate enhances learning and memory (2010)
  • Magtein improves brain cognitive functions in healthy adults (Zhang et al. 2022)

L-Theanine: Calm Focus & Stress Resilience

Our Dose: 150mg Focus / 200mg Restore (Suntheanine®)

Promotes calm focus without drowsiness; supports the evening transition

Research:

  • L-Theanine reduces stress-related symptoms and improves cognitive function (Hidese et al. 2019)

L-Ornithine: Cortisol Clearing & Recovery

Our Dose: 400mg

Supports stress recovery and the transition from work mode to presence mode

Research:

  • L-Ornithine reduces cortisol and improves sleep quality in healthy workers (Miyake et al. 2014)

Saffron (affron®): Mood & Evening Presence

Our Dose: 28mg (affron®)

Supports positive mood and the quality of your evening experience

Research:

  • affron improves mood in healthy adults over 4 weeks (Kell et al. 2017)
  • Evening saffron extract improves sleep quality and melatonin (Lopresti et al. 2021)

Bacopa monnieri: Memory & Processing Speed

Our Dose: 300mg (50% bacosides)

Supports memory consolidation, cognitive processing speed, and attention

Research:

  • Bacopa enhances word recall and reduces anxiety in elderly (Calabrese et al. 2008)
  • Meta-analysis: Bacopa improves cognitive processing speed across 9 RCTs (Kongkeaw et al. 2014)

Lion's Mane: Neuroplasticity & Nerve Growth

Our Dose: 500mg (30% polysaccharides)

Supports Nerve Growth Factor production and long-term cognitive resilience

Research:

  • Lion's Mane improves cognitive function in mild cognitive impairment (Mori et al. 2009)
  • Acute and chronic effects on cognition and mood in young adults (Docherty et al. 2023)

Rhodiola rosea: Stress Adaptation & Mental Stamina

Our Dose: 150mg (3% rosavins, 1% salidroside)

Supports mental performance and resilience under stress

Research:

  • Rhodiola improves cognitive function during stressful night duty (Darbinyan et al. 2000)

Nicotinamide Riboside (Niagen®): Cellular Energy

Our Dose: 250mg (Niagen®)

Supports NAD+ replenishment and cellular energy metabolism

Research:

  • NR achieves 2.6-fold increase in blood NAD+ in older adults with MCI (Orr et al. 2023)
